About This Project

Chemistry is one of the most fundamental branches of science as it explains the composition, properties, and transformations of matter.

In every chemistry laboratory, numerous chemicals are employed for experimentation, research, and practical applications.

Chemicals are utilized to initiate reactions, prepare solutions, conduct analyses, and study various phenomena.

Understanding laboratory chemicals involves knowledge of their properties, functions, applications, hazards, and safety protocols.
